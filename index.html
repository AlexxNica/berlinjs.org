Berlin.JS is a usergroup focused on Javascript and related topics. We meet regularly on the 3rd Thursday each month at 7p.m. at co.up Offices, Adalbertstraße 7-8 in Berlin-Kreuzberg.

wru — unit tests have never been that easy

Andrea Giammarchi
@WebReflection

Due to the dynamic nature of JavaScript and it's different implementations testing your applications can be a bit of a pain.

Andrea is going to introduce us to wru, a small library for writing quick, portable and simple unit tests.

The art of waiting in JavaScript

Jakub Siberski @siberskj

Waiting in JavaScript comes in different ways, each with different purposes and caveats.

Jakub is going to show three standard ways to wait in JavaScript: active loop, setTimeout/setInterval & promises. As an extra he's going to present us his own implementation and the reasoning behind that.

The adventure of releasing a HTML & JS game in various app stores

Rene Dudfield
@renedudfield

Rene wanted to publish a halloween game in various app stores. Simple, you'd think. But, as usual with cross platform development there were several hurdles to overcome.

He will show us things that made him cry trying to upload my files. Bugs he had to work around, workflows he had to develop and boring forms he had to fill in before they would stamp his app as 'ok' to sell on the stores. And how he failed to get it onto some platforms in time.

How to convert your localStorage-based app into a remoteStorage-based app

Michiel de Jong
@unhosted

In this very short lightning talk Michiel is going to show us how easy it is to convert your localStorage based app into a remoteStorage app using the unhosted project.
