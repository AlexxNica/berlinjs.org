NodeJs Exposed - A Journey To The Bottom Of NodeJs

Bodo Kaiser

Have you always asked yourself how NodeJs is built up? Which components are involved to put it a live. How actually NodeJs is beeing non-blocking by only using a "single" thread? If yes I hope I can answer your questions in "NodeJs Exposed - A Journey To The Bottom Of NodeJs". I will talk mainly about libuv but I also will cut on topics like the http_parser or the v8 bindings.

WebRTC: Theory and Practice

Jan Lelis and Marius Melzer

The emerging WebRTC standard has the potential to change the internet as we know it: It enables new kinds of user experience and, moreover, pushes the web further from decentralized to distributed organization!

We are developing a video communication platform and want to share our lessons learned so far. We will take a look at the theoretical foundations and protocols that enable WebRTC. We will then demonstrate how you can already use WebRTC in your next project.
