Berlin.JS is a usergroup focused on Javascript and related topics. We meet regularly on the 3rd Thursday each month at 7p.m. at Co.Up Offices, Adalbertstraße 7-8 in Berlin-Kreuzberg.

The tickets were sold in a rush. Now things are getting serious.

What?

JSConf EU is awesome and we all wished we could speak and tell the world what awesome stuff we are working on. Alas, they could only pick so many speakers and many of us don't get to speak.
Reject.js is here for you!

When?

Reject.js will be held on Thursday, September 29th, 2011. We'll publish more details when we have them on our Twitter account @berlinjs. If you have any questions, Twitter is the best way to get in touch with us as well.

Who?

Reject.js is organised by BERLIN.JS, Berlin's finest JavaScript User Group as a community event. We can't pay for any speakers or travel, but we can show you a good JavaScript time!
