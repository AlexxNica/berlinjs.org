Berlin.JS is a usergroup focused on Javascript and related topics. We meet regularly on the 3rd Thursday each month at 7p.m. at co.up Offices, Adalbertstraße 7-8 in Berlin-Kreuzberg.

Next meetup on February 16th

3D transforms - Adding depth to the DOM

Felix Niklas @mrflix

Felix is gonna introduce us to the new hot and upcoming 3D CSS techniques.

In addition to that he will present a gimmick he's currently working on using the upcoming standard.

Lightweight client-side development with Brunch

Jan Monschke @thedeftone

Jan will show us Brunch, a framework that helps developing complex client-side applications using Backbone.js.
