Berlin.JS is a usergroup focused on JavaScript and related topics. We meet regularly on the 3rd Thursday each month at 7p.m. at co.up Offices, Adalbertstraße 7-8 in Berlin-Kreuzberg.

Next meetup on October 16th

Avoiding Burnout, and Other Essentials of Open Source Self Care

Kathleen Danielson

How to recognize the riskfactors and deal with burnout.

In this talk we will discuss burnout; learning how to identify it, understanding the causes in open source communities, and finding healthy responses.

Integrate real time communication & collaboration capabilities into your apps with Moxtra

Chandra Tungathurthi

Do you want to integrate real-time communication and collaboration capabilities into your apps with just a few lines of code, for free? Chandra Tungathurthi, Developer, Searcher and Moxtra ambassador will give a demo and present you the Moxtra SDK which allows you to add all of these functionalities to your apps: chat, conferences calls, document editing and sharing.

We would love to show you these features and get your feedback on the Moxtra SDK.

Designing an A/B Testing Framework

Brian Egan

A/B Testing is a process that can help you make decisions about your products. But how do A/B testing systems actually work, what are they useful for, and what are some common pitfalls when designing and building one?

We'll discuss how we implemented our own custom A/B testing infrastructure at SoundCloud. This includes how to design experiments, how to assign users into A/B tests, how to modify the UI based on those assignments, and how we perform statistical analysis.

Code of Conduct

Long story short: Be awesome to each other.

Our goal is to have an awesome and inclusive community meetup where people meet, hang out together, chat, listen to talks, exchange ideas and make new friends. Any harmful or discriminating behaviour will not be tolerated and results in the offending person being expelled from the meetup.
