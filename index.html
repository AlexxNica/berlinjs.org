Berlin.JS is a usergroup focused on Javascript and related topics. We meet regularly on the 3rd Thursday each month at 7p.m. at Co.Up Offices, Adalbertstraße 7-8 in Berlin-Kreuzberg.

wru — unit tests have never been that easy

Andrea Giammarchi
@WebReflection

Due to the dynamic nature of JavaScript and it's different implementations testing your applications can be a bit of a pain.

Andrea is going to introduce us to wru, a small library for writing quick, portable and simple unit tests.

Back to regular awesomeness

After that great event called Reject.JS we're now going back to normal mode. That means: monthly meetups again, evenings packed with fine talks, the cosy atmosphere at our hosts place, the Co.Up Offices.

Step up and give your talk

We're currently looking for talks. Be it a full-fledged keynote or a five minute lightning talk, you're welcome to share your thoughts and findings on Javascript. To propose your topic just drop us a line on Twitter or our Mailinglist.

Next up on berlinjs.org

Besides our regular announcements we will evolve that website step by step. Soon we will add some features that we're considering helpful to the Javascript community.
