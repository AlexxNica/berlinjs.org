Berlin.JS is a usergroup focused on Javascript and related topics. We meet regularly on the 3rd Thursday each month at 7p.m. at co.up Offices, Adalbertstraße 7-8 in Berlin-Kreuzberg.

What the Santa?

Santa.JS is the christmas special edition of Berlin.JS. After a year of great meetups we would like to keep the last meetup a bit more informal than usual.

We plan to have just a handful of short talks within a cozy atmosphere while having some mulled wine, cookies and pastries.

But there is no christmas without gifts and traditions. So we invented Javascript Julklap for this particular occasion.

*Javascript Julklap?

Julklap is about anonymously giving gifts to random members of a group.
Javascript Julklap is a bit different. The participants will give randomly assigned talks to the community. Nobody knows who will get and give which talk.

Javascript Julklap is derived from german christmas tradition known as "Juklap" or "Wichteln". If you are not familiar with it, you may read about it here (english) or there (german).

How does it work?

The plan for Santa.JS in a few words:

  1. There are only lightning talks
  2. Topic assignments are randomized
  3. You'd like to give talk? Sign up here.
  4. Don't worry, topics won't be overly challenging
  5. Big fun on December 15th.
