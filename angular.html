AngularJS Meetup Berlin

The AngularJS Meetup Berlin is a meetup around the AngularJS framework. We're open to anyone. Since this is such a specific topic the meetings are very informal and ad-hoc. Whether you're a beginner dying to poke questions at someone with experience or a veteran wanting to discuss the finer details, come along. We can help with specific questions for your problem, write or review some code together.

We usually don't have presentations. Whatever happens at the meetups is shaped by all participants. Ask questions, show us your code or just chat with us.

Join us on Wednesday August 14, 2013 around 7:30pm for the fifth AngularJS Meetup Berlin.

It will take place at the co.up coworking space, Adalberstr. 8, 10999 Berlin

There’s an event page on lanyrd.com you might join to acknowledge that you’re attending (this is not mandatory).
