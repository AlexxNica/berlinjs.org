Berlin.JS Special Event: Kiezcoding

Daytime co-working pre-Meetup event

Kiezcoding is a unique and fun way to change up your regular work routine. You can have the chance to work alongside other friends (and make new ones!) in the Berlin.JS community!

Join us at co.up on Thursday, April 21 at any time, starting from 10:00.

Bring your laptop, your coworkers, and your friends to co.up for a fun, productive day to get your work done outside of your regular workspace! Pair programming and helping each other do better work by solving problems together and sharing your knowledge is highly encouraged, but not enforced.

The space has wifi, desk space, and plenty of power outlets for you.

We'll spend the day working together, and finish up the evening with Berlin’s longest running JavaScript meetup, BerlinJS.

As always, our events adhere to the Berlin CoC.

There’s an event page on Meetup.com you might join to acknowledge that you’re attending (this is not mandatory).

When is it?

Thursday, April 21
10:00 -- Doors open
10:15 - 10:30 -- Introductions and Opening Announcements
10:30 - 19:00 -- Co-working
19:00 - 21:30 -- Berlin.JS

You are also welcome to come at any time

How much is it?

Regular co-working day passes at co.up are 15 euro per day. For our special event, there will be a need-based sliding scale from 0-15 euro. If you are able to, register for a regular priced co.up day pass. If you are not able to pay the full price for any reason, email us to make the arrangements.

Who should come?

Anyone who wants to spend a day working alongside other BerlinJS community members. Your work does not have to be related to JavaScript to participate.
