Berlin.JS Special Event co-hosted by Sauce Labs

Selenium & Cloud-Based Testing

If you've given any thought to automated web testing, then this is the Berlin.JS meetup for you! Join us April 24th for a special event co-hosted with Sauce Labs as they discuss Selenium and the benefits of cloud-based automated testing.
Get your free ticket!

Automated Testing – State of the Union

Adam Christian
Director of Web Development

This talk will be an overview in the recent developments in automated testing, new technologies and methodology being used.

We'll cover reactions on what works and what doesn't and what the next year or two will bring; the kind of solutions being developed for mobile browser, and native mobile automation; and the status of the Selenium Project from a community perspective and the emerging excitement from the Node.JS community when it comes to testing.

The Ins and Outs of Selenium

Santiago Suarez Ordonez
Sauce Ninja

In this interactive talk that is meant to work for audiences of different skill levels, we'll cover the basics of the Selenium Project, usage and architecture, and then move to more advanced topics, such as test optimizations and general tricks and tips to get the most out of Selenium and Sauce Labs.

Keyword driven testing w/ Robot Framework (and Selenium)

Filip Noetzel
Does computer stuff.

Robot Framework is a generic keyword-driven testrunner that allows to combine almost all popular test styles ( ATDD, BDD, DDD). Test cases are written in a simple, semicolon-free keyword-style syntax, and there is premium selenium support.

Using selenium tests as an example, the talk will give in introduction to the problem, show how you how to write Robot Framework tests, how to organize your tests into layers (and why that's a bloody good idea!).
